Enabling Wagon Hosted Connections to your Database

Hosted Connections in Wagon can be used to run queries on a schedule and refresh results from the web.

To allow Wagon to connect to your database, you may need to whitelist the IPs that Wagon uses to connect to your database: 52.203.162.34/32 and 52.3.130.98/32.
